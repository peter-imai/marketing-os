# Data-Driven ICP Generation Prompt

Your task is to create a highly detailed Ideal Customer Profile by analyzing company data and conducting thorough web research. Focus on extracting specific, quantifiable characteristics that define their most successful customers.

## Research Instructions

First, gather data from these sources in this order:

1. Company Website Analysis
    - Product pages: Note technical requirements, integration capabilities
    - Case studies: Extract customer company sizes, industries, use cases
    - Customer logos/testimonials: Research these companies on LinkedIn
    - Pricing pages: Identify minimum deal sizes, package tiers
    - Partner ecosystem: Analyze technology requirements, complementary tools
2. Web Research
    - G2 and Capterra reviews: Note company sizes and industries of reviewers
    - LinkedIn: Research customers mentioned in case studies
    - Crunchbase: Analyze funding and size of customer companies
    - TechCrunch/Business Wire: Find press releases about customer wins
    - Company leadership LinkedIn profiles: Note target market mentions
    - Job postings: Identify target market focus in sales roles
3. Technology Footprint
    - BuiltWith: Analyze technology stack requirements
    - Integration marketplace: Document required technical capabilities
    - API documentation: Determine technical sophistication needed

## Required ICP Components

Based on the research, provide specific details for each category:

### Company Size Parameters

- Exact annual revenue range with evidence
- Employee count range with evidence
- Department sizes (particularly for target users)
- Growth rate ranges of current customers
- Minimum budget requirements with evidence

### Industry Details

- Primary industry verticals with percentages
- Sub-industries with specific use cases
- Regulatory requirements by industry
- Competitive dynamics in each vertical
- Industry-specific pain points

### Technical Requirements

- Required technology stack components
- Integration requirements
- Data volume handling capabilities
- Security certifications needed
- IT team size and expertise needed

### Budget and Purchase Authority

- Typical contract values
- Budget holder positions
- Purchase approval chains
- Implementation budget requirements
- ROI expectations with timeframes

### Team Structure

- Required departments
- Key stakeholder roles
- Minimum team sizes
- Expertise levels needed
- Reporting relationships

### Business Metrics

- Growth rates
- Customer base size
- Transaction volumes
- Data processing requirements
- Performance benchmarks

### Use Case Specifics

- Primary problems solved
- Required business processes
- Implementation complexity
- Time to value ranges
- Success metrics

### Geographical Focus

- Primary market locations
- Language requirements
- Regulatory considerations
- Support timezone needs
- Local market presence requirements

## Output Format

Provide the ICP details in this structure:

1. Core Metrics
    - All specific numerical ranges with sources
    - Market size calculations
    - Growth metrics
    - Budget ranges
    - Team size requirements
2. Technical Profile
    - Technology stack requirements
    - Integration needs
    - Data capabilities
    - Security requirements
    - Infrastructure needs
3. Business Profile
    - Industry details
    - Use case specifications
    - Team structures
    - Process requirements
    - Success metrics
4. Market Position
    - Competitive landscape
    - Growth stage
    - Market penetration
    - Geographic focus
    - Industry standing

For each detail provided, include:

- Specific numerical ranges where applicable
- Source of information
- Confidence level based on data availability
- Supporting examples from research

Remember to:

- Use actual numbers instead of vague terms
- Include specific job titles and roles
- Quote actual customer examples
- Provide market sizes and segments
- Detail technology requirements
- Specify exact budget ranges
- Include all quantifiable metrics found
